Record Clerk: Music Challenge

Description

Record Clerk: Music Challenge integrates Apple Music and iTunes to create music trivia challenges for all genres and eras of music.

Features

  • 100% Free! No in app purchases or in game currency.
  • Hundreds of challenges featuring thousands of songs.
  • Enjoy the music! Score is based on accuracy. Players are not rushed with timers.
  • High quality photos to set the mood for each challenge.
  • Genres include: Alt Rock, Rock, Country, Hip Hop, Top Hits, R&B
  • Bonus challenges will test how well you know the deep cuts from your favorite genres.
  • New challenges added with regular updates.
